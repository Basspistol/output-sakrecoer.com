During a few months somewhere around 2003, a good friend of mine lent me this amazing device. Very quickly i got very fond of it. The built sounds are cheezy but also very punchy. It didn’t take very long before i had few dozens of track made on it. It’s rough and naive, the sound is shit, but i’m still very happy about the output.

I hope you will like it too.
